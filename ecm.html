COURSE SAMPLE

Overview

Context

Type: Learner Experience Design

Duration: June 2018 - Feb 2019

Team: Armando, Patty, Steve, Charity

Topic: E-Commerce Category Management

Working at an e-learning company that strives in successful client relations with unique, learner-friendly courses, I carried out various tasks for different clients, instructors, and internal projects. Some projects include App Icon & Logo project, and prototyping for learner platform UI, Category Analyst course UI and Titleist Golf Ball course. For this specific case study, we'll be exploring the main project I worked on, eCommerce Category Management Course, a fun online training course I assisted in establishing the contents, look, and feel.


Problem

How might we develop an innovative, interactive course with originality and compelling story-telling?

As eCommerce is emerging, the job titles of category management has been on the rise. For old and new category managers, it's crucial for them to learn about what is on their plate as retailers switch from brick & mortar to eCommerce. To approach a unique style, Patricia and I ideated the concept of pop art/graphic novel style to engage learners to interact and understand the material in a fun way. For this to happen, we needed to plan a thought-out design process to successfully launch the course.
