POKI YAKI

Quick, easy way to enjoy a tasty customized meal

VIEW WEBSITE

Overview

Context

Type: Web & Service Design

Duration: June 2015 - Aug 2017

This is an individual project completed on-site (service) and remotely (web) for Poki Yaki, a poke restaurant in Orange County established during summer of 2015. Since the poke bowl trend started booming a few years ago, it was important to set foundation in various aspects of design for brand reputation and business recognition. Customers can personalize their poke bowls with choices of various base, fish, toppings, and sauce. This ordering system was an unfamiliar idea to many people back in 2015, so the owner, manager, and I worked together to design the experience with a holistic view. Also since early 2018, I assisted in branding Poki Yaki's affiliate company, Poke Time, through logo and menu design while also creating promotional materials and managing social media pages, such as Facebook and Instagram.


Problem

How might we design an ordering experience that leads to a fast, efficient service for overall customer satisfaction?

Poke is recognized as the new food trend. Within its ordering procedure, it's crucial for the process to be quick, simple, and easy-to-follow for both new and returning customers. With these 3 core objectives in mind and by interacting with customers, I worked closely with the owner and manager to create an efficient service design that gives special attention to customers' needs and frustrations. After the foundation was set, I was able to design the store website with visualization of the menu layout. I also created marketing collaterals with digital and print designs.
